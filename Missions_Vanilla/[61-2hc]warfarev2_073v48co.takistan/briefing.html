WASP Warfare Release Candidate

BLUFOR and OPFOR fight for the island. Capture towns, build factories, research upgrades, and destroy the enemy HQ. Both sides are led by a commander who controls base construction and upgrade research.

Resources

Cash — buys units, vehicles and gear. Both sides start with $11,600.
Supply — used by the commander to build factories and structures. Both sides start with 7,400 supply.

Capturing Towns

Take the strongpoint bunkers around a town first, then hold numerical superiority at the town depot (50 m circle) to capture it. Captured strongpoints are respawn points within range of the town.
Capture grace period: after capture, 5 minutes before enemy AI respawn spawns at the town; lingering enemy defenders have 3 minutes to clear before the timer expires.

Release Candidate - Headline Changes

Bank (endgame objective)

The commander can build the Federal Reserve / Bank Rossii (9,500 supply; must be placed at least 800 m from your HQ/base area, one per side). While your HQ stands it drips a $6,000 pool every 5 minutes split among living players. Destroying the enemy bank pays +10,000 side supply and $25,000 to the killer. Both banks are marked on the map for both sides.

Counter Battery Radar (CBR)

Buildable structure (2,400 supply) that detects enemy artillery firing positions and marks them for 75 seconds. Requires your Anti-Air Radar to be alive. The CBR Radar upgrade extends detection radius: 750 m → 1,500 m → 2,000 m. Each captured airfield provides a permanent indestructible 2,000 m CBR.

Airfields

NWAF, NEAF and Balota Airfield are now live capture objectives (40 SV garrisons, defended by PMC infantry and vehicles). Capturing an airfield gives your side a repair point and an exclusive hangar with aircraft unavailable elsewhere (L-39, An-2, Mi-17 variants on Chernarus).

Capture-to-Unlock Premium Units

Holding Krasnostav unlocks the Czech T-72 (Heavy Factory level 4, $7,000).
Holding NW Airfield unlocks the RM-70 rocket artillery (Light Factory level 4, $6,800; fully integrated into the artillery fire mission menu).
Both unlock for the holding side only and are available at your own factories while you hold the trigger town.

Patrols and Convoys

Research the Patrols upgrade (4 levels; supply cost 300 / 1,600 / 2,400 / 3,200). Patrols spawn near your HQ and push toward the frontline capturing as they go. Maximum 3 active patrols per side.
Level 4 — Convoys: each patrol fields a supply truck that pays your team $750 split equally at every town stop.
Running patrols reduces every player's max AI count by 1.

Factory Queue and Buy Menu

Queue depth is shown as N/CAP in the buy menu. Caps scale with factory level but have minimum floors: Barracks 10, Light Factory 5, Heavy/Aircraft 3. You can cancel the last queued order for a partial refund.

Medic Redeployment Truck

Purchasable at the Light Factory (violet row). Medics only — acts as a forward spawn point when parked with the engine off, a free cargo seat, and at least 500 m from any non-friendly town.

Classes

Your class and its abilities are shown on join and are re-readable via the Class Info action in your action menu. Class tags (SOL/SUP/MED/ENG/SNI) appear on the map and in the Notes tab.

Other Changes

  • EASA loadout editor tags each loadout [AA], [AG] or [MR] (multirole) so you know what you are switching to.
  • Rearm cost is proportional to ammo missing (10 % floor; artillery exempt).
  • WDDM commander compositions are capped at 3 per base area. Over-cap attempts refund the cash.
  • Defense budgets cap statics, fortifications and mines per category (scales with barracks level). Statics and mines are blocked if 3+ enemy ground units are inside base range.
  • Tanks and wheeled APCs come crewed by engineers.
  • Earplugs toggle now also fades radio and voice volume, and works while mounted in a vehicle.
