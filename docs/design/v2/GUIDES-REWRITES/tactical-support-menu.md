# Tactical Support Menu

The **Tactical Support Menu** gives your team battlefield tools when the commander and mission state allow them.

## Before You Call Support

Confirm the target. Check friendly positions. Call support where it changes the fight, not where it only makes noise.

## Good Targets

Use support against hardened towns, enemy armor groups, exposed base structures, and routes the enemy must cross.

## Bad Targets

Do not call support on unclear contacts, friendly-held towns, or targets already collapsing. Save support for the next hard position.

## After The Strike

Move immediately. Support creates the opening; infantry and vehicles take the ground.

## Team Communication

Announce the target, timing, and danger area in team chat or voice before the strike lands.
