# Supply Missions

**Supply Runs** turn movement into team power. They fund upgrades, structures, and recovery after heavy losses.

## Start A Run

Use the **WF Menu** or the logistics interface shown by the mission. Take the assigned vehicle, follow the route, and deliver the supplies.

## Protect The Truck

Drive with cover. Avoid main roads when the enemy controls nearby towns. Ask for escort when the route crosses the front.

## Deliver

Reach the destination and complete the delivery. The team gains **Supplies Delivered** credit and the economy improves.

## When To Run Supplies

Run supplies when upgrades are delayed, the commander is rebuilding, or the team has captured towns faster than the economy can support.

## Common Mistakes

Do not abandon the truck in the open. Do not drive through a known killzone twice. Do not run supplies alone through an active enemy town.
