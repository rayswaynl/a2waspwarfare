# Gameplay Loop

WASP is a capture-and-economy war. Your team captures towns, earns income, buys units, builds structures, and pushes toward the enemy HQ.

## Capture

Move on towns with enough force to survive the first contact. Clear defenders, secure camps, and hold the area until the town flips.

## Build

The **Commander** builds the base and buys upgrades. Protect factories and service points. A strong base keeps the front supplied after the first wave dies.

## Spend

Use income. Buy infantry when you need bodies, vehicles when you need speed, armor when the route is open, and air when your team can protect it.

## Supply

Run supplies when upgrades stall or the base needs construction money. Escorts matter. A supply truck that arrives is worth more than a lone vehicle kill.

## Escalate

Early rounds favor infantry and light vehicles. Mid rounds bring armor, aircraft, artillery, and deeper raids. Late rounds punish exposed HQs and weak logistics.

## Win

Keep pressure on towns, destroy the enemy's ability to rebuild, and finish the HQ when the route is open.
