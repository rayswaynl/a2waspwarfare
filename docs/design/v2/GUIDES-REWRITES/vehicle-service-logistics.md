# Vehicle Service And Logistics

Vehicles decide tempo when they stay repaired, armed, and fueled. Service them before the next fight.

## Service Points

Use **Service Points** to repair, rearm, and refuel. Pull damaged vehicles out before they burn. A repaired vehicle returns pressure to the front.

## EASA Menu

Use the **EASA Menu** for aircraft service and loadout work. Land cleanly, service quickly, and leave the runway open.

## Convoy Discipline

Move vehicles in pairs when the route is contested. Infantry clears towns; vehicles control roads and punish exposed targets.

## Recovery

Recover valuable vehicles when the front moves. Do not leave armor, aircraft, or support vehicles where the enemy can destroy or capture them.

## Logistics Priority

Repair the HQ, supply vehicles, factories, and high-value armor before replacing disposable vehicles.
