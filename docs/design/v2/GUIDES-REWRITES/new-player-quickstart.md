# New Player Quickstart

Join a side, secure your gear, and move with your team. WASP is a Warfare round: **NATO**, **CSAT**, and the **Insurgents** fight for towns, income, and the HQ.

## First Spawn

Open the **Gear Menu** and take a practical rifle, launcher, map tools, medical supplies, and enough ammunition for one fight. Stay near your squad until you understand the front line.

## Your First Objective

Capture towns. Towns generate income, open movement routes, and pull the round toward your side. Secure the camps first, then hold the town long enough for control to flip.

## Spend Your Money

Use the **WF Menu** to buy units, vehicles, and equipment. Do not hoard money while your team loses ground. Spend on the next fight.

## Protect the HQ

The **HQ** is your team's build anchor. If the HQ is exposed, warn your team and help move, repair, or defend it. A lost HQ changes the whole round.

## Use Logistics

Run supplies when the front stalls. **Supply Runs** feed upgrades and structures. A protected supply route wins long rounds.

## Read the Fight

Artillery, air, armor, and infantry all matter. Move after firing. Avoid isolated attacks. Join massed pushes and defend towns your team just captured.

## First-Round Checklist

1. Pick a side.
2. Gear up.
3. Join a squad or move with friendly markers.
4. Capture camps before the town.
5. Spend money on the next fight.
6. Protect the HQ.
7. Run supplies if the commander needs income.
