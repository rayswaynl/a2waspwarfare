# WASP B59 → B60 Improvement Plan

## Headline
B59 (15-team load test) is healthy on the box — server FPS 38–42 this cycle (floor ~28 at ~460 units / 8 active towns), zero script errors, founding at the 15/side cap, Mi-24_P flying & scoring kills, no crashes. But the round does not *progress*: the front pushed from 2-2 up to a **6–6 symmetric parity stall** (held=6/enemyHeld=6, contested=0, distFront pinned at Guglovo 1348 / Stary Sobor 1126 — never moves), unitsPerTeam pinned ~5.5–6.8 both sides (the bleed-with-no-recovery ratchet, now symmetric). **The new dominant live signal is a WEST-side assault-mobility stall:** STUCKSTAT 90 WEST vs 3 EAST, ASSAULT_STRANDED 10/10 WEST, recurring leaders `USMC_Soldier_Crew`/`US_Soldier_AR_EP1`, `distStart=0`/`moved=0` — WEST teams literally never leave spawn, so WEST cannot project force forward and the front freezes. Two structural defects now have hard live evidence and rank near the top:

1. **The HC-team bleed has no recovery path AND no cleanup path.** Depleted teams (`alive≤1`) far from HQ are re-ordered to "retreat-and-reform" *every CMDR cycle forever* (live: EAST [O 1-2-F] 67× at 7.8km, WEST [B 1-2-A] 53×) — but the order block has no state guard (re-fires endlessly), never refills (refill is disabled there and skipped for 100% of HC teams anyway), and there is **no disband path keyed on alive count**, so the dead slot is never reclaimed and the side stays below team-strength. This is the mechanical engine of the unitsPerTeam bleed + the 67×/53× log spam + wasted commander cycles.
2. **The unstuck ladder dead-ends for foot leaders with no road nearby.** Marksman/MG leaders sit at `distStart=0`, `strike=5`, reissued forever — because the Tier-3 foot teleport silently no-ops when no road is within 150m (woods/hilltop/field — exactly where A2 PFM strands infantry). The strike counter is then *zeroed* by the abandon-blacklist, so it can never accumulate to drive a terminal escalation, and ASSAULT_STRANDED is silenced by the same blacklist resetting its clock.

Above both, the strategy lever stands: the **memoryless spearhead picker** (`AI_Commander_Strategy.sqf:107-155`) re-scores deterministically every ~60s with zero progress memory, so the same town is re-selected forever and the front never breaks. A newly-surfaced strategy defect compounds it: the **aggression gate misreads garrison troops as available strength** — the DEFEND gate (`Strategy.sqf:388-399`) compares *total* side strength, so a territorial leader that garrisons many towns sees its *maneuver* strength dribble below the concentrated enemy and **goes passive while winning** — the exact 2-2 stalemate signature (#13). The one risk to watch across all of this: never introduce a standing-still or front-teleported AI; the MHQ relocation is the single item that can lose a round outright.

**⚠ Most urgent this cycle — B60 is NOT deployable as built.** Source review (cycle 2) found the BUILT `AI_Commander_MHQReloc.sqf` (commit `85653acc`) does NOT implement this plan's #11 guards: it triggers on front >2500m (no upper hop cap), its destination is forward of the current HQ, and its deadline/stuck fallback `setPos`-teleports the MHQ **FORWARD toward the front** — gated only on no-player-900m, with a STALE enemy check. That is the single round-losing action the invariant forbids, shipped default-ON. **B60 = HOLD; MHQ must be re-built to spec and defaulted OFF before it can even be trialed** (full detail in B60 go/no-go below).
