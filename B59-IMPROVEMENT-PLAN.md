# WASP B59 → B60 Improvement Plan

## Headline
B59 (15-team load test) is healthy on the box — server FPS 30–47 (floor ~30 at ~550 units / 11 active towns), zero script errors, founding at the 15/side cap, no crashes. But the round does not *progress*: the front is a clean **2–2 stalemate** (WEST holds Nizhnoye+Dolina, EAST holds NWAF+Grishino, 0 contested) and **EAST's unitsPerTeam is bleeding down** (6.2→5.9→5.6) while WEST holds ~7.3–7.9. Two structural defects now have hard live evidence and rank near the top:

1. **The HC-team bleed has no recovery path AND no cleanup path.** Depleted teams (`alive≤1`) far from HQ are re-ordered to "retreat-and-reform" *every CMDR cycle forever* (live: EAST [O 1-2-F] 67× at 7.8km, WEST [B 1-2-A] 53×) — but the order block has no state guard (re-fires endlessly), never refills (refill is disabled there and skipped for 100% of HC teams anyway), and there is **no disband path keyed on alive count**, so the dead slot is never reclaimed and the side stays below team-strength. This is the mechanical engine of the unitsPerTeam bleed + the 67×/53× log spam + wasted commander cycles.
2. **The unstuck ladder dead-ends for foot leaders with no road nearby.** Marksman/MG leaders sit at `distStart=0`, `strike=5`, reissued forever — because the Tier-3 foot teleport silently no-ops when no road is within 150m (woods/hilltop/field — exactly where A2 PFM strands infantry). The strike counter is then *zeroed* by the abandon-blacklist, so it can never accumulate to drive a terminal escalation, and ASSAULT_STRANDED is silenced by the same blacklist resetting its clock.

Above both, the strategy lever stands: the **memoryless spearhead picker** (`AI_Commander_Strategy.sqf:107-155`) re-scores deterministically every ~60s with zero progress memory, so the same town is re-selected forever and the front never breaks. The one risk to watch across all of this: never introduce a standing-still or front-teleported AI; the MHQ relocation is the single item that can lose a round outright.
