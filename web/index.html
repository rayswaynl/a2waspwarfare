THEATRE / DIFFICULTY
Field Manual
  • Select — left-click a unit, or drag a box to select many.
  • Move / Attack — right-click the ground or an enemy.
  • Capture — park units on a neutral or enemy town until the flag flips.
  • Buy — pick a factory tab on the right, then click a unit to queue it. It spawns at your HQ.
  • Economy — towns pay Funds each tick; supply depots raise your Supply cap.
  • Win — capture the enemy HQ or hold every town. Lose your HQ and it's over.
  • Hotkeys — Space pause · Tab cycle idle · 1-5 factory tabs · H centre HQ
