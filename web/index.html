Field Manual
  • Select — left-click a unit, or drag a box to select many.
  • Move / Attack — right-click the ground or an enemy.
  • Capture — park units on a neutral or enemy town until the flag flips.
  • Buy — pick a factory tab on the right, then click a unit to queue it. It spawns at your HQ (double-click the map to set a forward rally).
  • Veterancy — units earn chevrons from kills, hitting harder and tankier.
  • Support powers — Z artillery · X airstrike · C paradrop · V smoke, then click a target.
  • Strategic points — capture the Radar (reveals fog), Oil (income) and Repair Depot (heals) dotted across the map.
  • Fog & weather — you only see what your forces scout; rain, sandstorms and nightfall cut your sight. Forests hide infantry.
  • Defenses & upgrades — the BASE tab places emplacements; the UPGRADE tab buys army-wide tech.
  • Win — capture the enemy HQ or hold every town (or the town majority, in Domination).
  • Hotkeys — Q W E R T Y tabs · Ctrl+1-9/1-9 groups · O outpost · Tab idle · H HQ · Space pause · arrows pan
  • On a phone — tap a unit to select, tap the ground/enemy to order it; drag to pan, pinch to zoom, long-press a unit to grab all of its type. Use the side buttons: ▢ box-select · ■ stop · ⌂ centre HQ · ≡ show/hide panel.
